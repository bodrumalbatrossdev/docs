## Market Overview

Gündoğan, a charming coastal village nestled on the Bodrum Peninsula, has emerged as a significant player in Turkey's real estate market. Located between Yalıkavak and Türkbükü, Gündoğan offers a blend of historical charm and modern amenities, making it an attractive destination for both domestic and international investors. The village's rich history, dating back to 4 BC, and its strategic location on the Aegean Sea contribute to its growing appeal ([VisitTurkey, 2024](https://visitturkey.in/district/gundogan-bodrum/)).

### Current State and Trends

The real estate market in Gündoğan has witnessed substantial growth in recent years, driven by a mix of economic factors and local developments. According to Endeksa, the average price for residential properties in Bodrum, including Gündoğan, was approximately 14,587,440 TL in November 2024, reflecting a 24.66% increase over the past year ([Bodrum Holiday, 2024](https://www.bodrumholiday.com/real-estate-agencies-in-bodrum/)). This growth is attributed to high demand from both domestic and international buyers, improved infrastructure, and rising construction costs.

Gündoğan's natural beauty and outdoor activities, such as water sports and sailing, further enhance its attractiveness. The village's calm waters and favorable winds make it an ideal spot for activities like windsurfing, sailing, and diving, catering to a wide range of outdoor enthusiasts ([VisitTurkey, 2024](https://visitturkey.in/district/gundogan-bodrum/)).

## Price Analysis

### Average Prices by Property Type

The average price per square meter in Bodrum, including Gündoğan, is $4,500. A 1-bedroom property of 60 square meters would cost approximately $270,000, and a 2-bedroom property of 85 square meters would cost approximately $383,000 ([Investropa, 2024](https://investropa.com/blogs/news/bodrum-property)). However, prices can fluctuate significantly depending on the property's characteristics and location.
