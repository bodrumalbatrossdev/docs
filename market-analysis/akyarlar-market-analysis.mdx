## Market Overview

### Current State and Trends

Bodrum, a picturesque town in Turkey, has been a significant player in the real estate market, reflecting the broader trends of the country's economy and stability. Historically, Turkey's economy has experienced fluctuations, impacting its real estate sector. Despite these ups and downs, the housing market, especially in areas like Bodrum, has often remained a point of interest for both local and international investors ([Investropa, 2024](https://investropa.com/blogs/news/bodrum-real-estate-market)).

### Akyarlar

Akyarlar, located 22 kilometers from Bodrum, is the southernmost point of the Bodrum Peninsula. Once a small fishing village, it has transformed into a popular tourist destination. The area is known for its beautiful bay with shallow and warm waters, making it an attractive spot for families and water sports enthusiasts ([Yes Bodrum, n.d.](http://www.yesbodrum.com/akyarlar_bodrum.html)).

### Aspat

Aspat, located near Akyarlar, is known for its stunning views and natural beauty. The Aspat Dağı (Aspat Mountain) offers panoramic views of the Aegean and Mediterranean seas, making it a popular spot for hiking and sightseeing ([Bodrum Ekspres, n.d.](https://www.bodrumekspres.com/gezi-rehberi/akyarlar-gezilecek-yerler.html)).

## Price Analysis

### Average Prices by Property Type

#### Residential Properties

The average cost of purchasing property in Bodrum is around $4,500 per square meter, according to the Central Bank of the Republic of Turkey ([Kaplankaya, 2023](https://kaplankaya.com/bodrum-property-investment/)). This price can vary significantly based on location and property type.

![](/ae9fed54-4b06-40c3-a011-8e533a8fd90a.png)

#### Akyarlar

Akyarlar offers a range of residential properties, from apartments to villas. The area is known for its affordable housing options compared to other parts of Bodrum. For instance, the price for a sun lounger at Aspat Beach is around 35 TL (€4.25), indicating a relatively lower cost of living ([Road is Calling, n.d.](https://www.roadiscalling.com/best-beaches-in-bodrum-peninsula/)).

#### Aspat

Aspat, being a more upscale area, tends to have higher property prices. The luxury villas and beachfront properties in this region command premium prices, reflecting the area's desirability and scenic beauty ([Kaplankaya, 2023](https://kaplankaya.com/bodrum-property-investment/)).

## Investment Potential

### Growth Indicators

#### Tourism

Bodrum's status as a premier tourist destination is a key driver of its real estate market. The area's vibrant culture, historic sites, and stunning coastline attract a steady stream of visitors, creating a strong demand for both rental and residential properties ([Investropa, 2024](https://investropa.com/blogs/news/bodrum-real-estate-market)).

#### Economic Stability

The Turkish government has made efforts to simplify the buying process for foreigners and enhance transparency, providing legal safeguards for investors. This has improved the stability and transparency of the legal framework surrounding real estate investment in Bodrum ([Investropa, 2024](https://investropa.com/blogs/news/bodrum-real-estate-market)).

### Risk Factors

#### Economic Fluctuations

Turkey's economy has historically experienced fluctuations, which can impact the real estate market. Investors should be aware of these economic risks and conduct thorough due diligence before making investment decisions ([Investropa, 2024](https://investropa.com/blogs/news/bodrum-real-estate-market)).

#### Market Saturation

Some areas of Bodrum, such as Yalıkavak and Türkbükü, have experienced significant growth and may be approaching market saturation. Investors should consider less saturated markets like Turgutreis and Gümüşlük for potential discounted properties ([Aegean Living & Investing, 2025](https://aegeanlivinginvesting.com/bodrum-real-estate-investment-2025/)).

## Future Projections

### 3-5 Year Market Outlook

Looking ahead to 2025, the Bodrum property market is expected to stabilize after a period of economic fluctuation. While some areas will continue to see premium prices, the economic downturn may offer investors a unique chance to secure properties at reduced prices, particularly in less saturated markets like Turgutreis and Gümüşlük ([Aegean Living & Investing, 2025](https://aegeanlivinginvesting.com/bodrum-real-estate-investment-2025/)).

### Value Appreciation

Despite economic challenges, Bodrum continues to see property value appreciation, particularly in well-known regions like Yalıkavak, Türkbükü, and Bitez. These areas have experienced steady growth over the past decade and remain popular among international buyers. As demand continues to grow, property values in these regions are expected to rise even further, making them excellent long-term investments ([Aegean Living & Investing, 2025](https://aegeanlivinginvesting.com/bodrum-real-estate-investment-2025/)).

## Conclusion

Akyarlar and Aspat offer unique investment opportunities within the Bodrum Peninsula. Akyarlar, with its affordable housing options and family-friendly atmosphere, is an attractive choice for those looking for a peaceful lifestyle. Aspat, on the other hand, offers luxury properties with stunning views, catering to a more upscale market. Both areas benefit from Bodrum's strong tourism industry and the government's efforts to enhance the legal framework for real estate investment. However, investors should be aware of economic risks and market saturation in certain areas. With careful consideration and thorough due diligence, Akyarlar and Aspat present compelling investment opportunities in the Bodrum real estate market.
